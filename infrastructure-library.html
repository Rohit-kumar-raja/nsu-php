Central Library

Spread over 56,000 SQ. FT.

The libraries at Amity are more like Integrated Knowledge Resource Centres that are stocked with over 2 lac books in total; periodicals, references, national and international journals, covering all aspects of academic studies and research materials.

Access to world's best online journals

Amity Library has over 17,000 online journals. Some of the online resources Amity students have access to are Ebsco Host, UGC-Infonet, INDEST-AICTE, IS1 and Delnet. All Amity students are members of Amizone-Amity Intranet Zone which allows them easy access to these journals & papers.
